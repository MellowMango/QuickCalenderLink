QuickerCalendar Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

Welcome to QuickerCalendar. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

1. Data Collection and Usage

QuickerCalendar collects and processes the following information:

  • Google Calendar access token (for authentication purposes)
  • Current webpage title and URL (only when you choose to create an event)
  • Event details you input (title, date, time, description)

2. Google Calendar Data Handling

Our extension:

  • Only creates events in your Google Calendar with your explicit permission
  • Uses OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
  • Does not store your Google credentials
  • Only requests the minimum necessary permissions to create calendar events

3. Data Retention

QuickerCalendar:

  • Does not store any personal data on our servers
  • Only temporarily processes data during event creation
  • Automatically clears authentication tokens when you sign out

4. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke calendar access at any time through Google Account settings
  • Request information about your data usage
  • Remove the extension and all associated data

5. Contact Information

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@quickercalendar.com

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
